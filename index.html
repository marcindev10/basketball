The Game of Basketball

The Best Sport on The Planet

Basketball in the nutshell:

  • 1891 - The game of basketball was created by Dr. James Naismith in Springfield, Massachusetts.
  • 1893 - The first international basketball match was organized due to efforts from the US Army and the YMCA.
  • 1895 - The first recorded basketball game between two college teams was organized.
  • 1898 - Professional leagues were forming, as were collegiate leagues.
  • 1909 - National Collegiate Athletic Association (NCAA) took over the game.
  • 1932 - The International Basketball Federation (FIBA) was formed.
  • 1936 - The work of FIBA on the international game helped to make basketball become an Olympic sport.
  • 1937 - The National basketball league (NBL) was formed.
  • 1939 - The first NCAA DI tournament was held.
  • 1946 - The Basketball Assosiation of America (BAA) was created.
  • 1949 - Merge of NBL and BAA created National Basketball Association (NBA)
